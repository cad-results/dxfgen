## Tips for Writing Descriptions

- Be specific about dimensions and units
- Mention coordinate positions when important
- Describe spatial relationships clearly
- Specify entity types when possible (lines, circles, arcs, etc.)
- Include layer information if needed
- Mention any special requirements (closed polylines, hatch patterns, etc.)

## Supported Entities

- **Lines (L)**: Straight line segments
- **Circles (C)**: Complete circles
- **Arcs (A)**: Circular arcs with start and end angles
- **Polylines (P)**: Connected line segments
- **Hatches (H)**: Filled regions

## Coordinate System

- Origin (0, 0) is typically at the center or bottom-left
- X-axis runs horizontally (positive to the right)
- Y-axis runs vertically (positive upward)
- Angles are measured counter-clockwise from positive X-axis
- Units default to millimeters unless specified
